Your Questions, Answered

Is this book only for co-founders?

While the book is written with co-founders in mind, the principles and frameworks are applicable to any leadership team, small business owner, or manager looking to build a more collaborative and resilient organization.

What makes this book different from other business books?

"Sailing & Scaling" focuses on the human side of building a business. It's not about quick hacks or growth-at-all-costs strategies. Instead, it provides a reflective, values-driven approach to leadership and partnership.
