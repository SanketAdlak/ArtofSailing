About the Author

Ravi Warrier

Ravi Warrier is a seasoned product leader, entrepreneur, and coach who specializes in helping early-stage companies navigate the complexities of co-founder dynamics and sustainable growth. With over 15 years of experience in the tech industry, Ravi has witnessed firsthand the challenges that ambitious founders face.

His work is guided by the belief that great companies are built on a foundation of trust, clear communication, and shared values. Through his writing and coaching, Ravi provides actionable frameworks that empower teams to build resilient partnerships and impactful businesses.

When he's not writing or working with startups, Ravi enjoys sailing and mentoring the next generation of entrepreneurs. He lives in Bangalore with his family.
