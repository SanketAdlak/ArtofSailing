About the Author

The Founder's Founder.

Ravi Warrier was building websites in 1995, the year the internet arrived in India. His 30-year journey from a 17-year-old entrepreneur to a pivotal figure in India's startup ecosystem is driven by one mission: to bridge the gap between brilliant founders and the right investors.

As the founder of Funding Pitchcraft and a resident mentor at premier incubators like IIIT Hyderabad, Ravi has been the trusted advisor inside the room for over 1,000 startups. He's not just a pitch coach; he's an architect of startup narratives, helping founders translate complex ideas into compelling stories that secure funding.

But beyond the metrics, Ravi operates as a student of human behavior and communication. He believes the toughest challenges in a startup aren't in the code or the business plan, but in the conversations between co-founders. This book is a distillation of that core philosophy.
