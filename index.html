Is this book only for venture-backed startups?

No. The frameworks were tested with bootstrapped, angel-backed, and VC-backed companies. If you have at least two founders and are hiring or fundraising, this book serves you.

Do we need a facilitator to use the worksheets?

Each toolkit includes facilitator notes. You can run the sessions yourselves or hand them to an operations leader to moderate.

What if we already worked with a coach?

Sailing & Scaling offers the structure to maintain momentum after coaching engagements end. Many founders use it to codify breakthroughs and onboarding new leaders.
