### **2\. Conversion Funnel Design: The India-Adapted Flow üáÆüá≥**

The website architecture is designed to manage the user journey sequentially, prioritizing validation and value before presenting the service price.

* A. Entry & Validation: The IP Consumer lands and immediately seeks proof and value.  
  Homepage ‚Üí Free Resources (Lead Magnet)  ‚Üí Testimonials/Success Stories (Social Proof)  
* B. Discovery & Intent: The user, now validated, explores the core business offerings.  
  Services (Work with Me) ‚Üí About/Credentials (Post-Intent Validation)  
* C. Decision & Intake: The client is ready to inquire, utilizing the least friction.  
  Client Inquires ‚Üí Low Friction ‚Üí Mandatory WhatsApp Floating Button

The funnel steps align with the strategic shift from **passive IP consumption** (Awareness) to **active Service Conversion** (Decision & Intake).

| Funnel Step | Audience Behavior | Key Action / Conversion Path |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **AWARENESS** | Reads Books, SM Posts, Webinars (Free Value). | **SM Posts/Ads** drive traffic to **Website/Free Resource Page**. |
| **INTEREST (Lead Capture)** | Curious; signs up for initial value (Lead Magnet). | **Action:** **Free WhatsApp Masterclass Signup** OR **Download "Startup Validation Checklist."** |
| **NURTURE** | Engaged lead receiving consistent value (7-14 days). | **Action:** Weekly **WhatsApp Messages** (Primary), Email sequence (Backup), Free webinar invitations. |
| **CONSIDERATION** | High intent; evaluating commitment/cost. | **Action:** Receive Case Study, **Book Purchase Nudge** (‚Çπ299-‚Çπ499), "Limited Seats" offer. |
| **DECISION & INTAKE** | Ready to invest. | **Action:** **Free 15-min Strategy Call** ‚Üí Coaching package revealed ‚Üí Application form (Qualify lead). |
| **CONVERSION** | Payment completed. | **Action:** UPI/Net Banking payment ‚Üí  Welcome to program ‚Üí  **Exclusive WhatsApp Group Invite** (Founders Circle / Founders 1-1 Sessions). |
